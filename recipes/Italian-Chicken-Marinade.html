Italian Chicken Marinade

Description

Indulge in the exquisite flavors of italian chicken, a tantalzing dish that is ensure to delight your taste buds and captivate your senses.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) bottle Italian-style salad dressing
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
